We are happy to announce that, Ripple Roo has been made official partnership with CryptoSky Platform. We invite you to take a closer look at our partners project.

Ripple Roo ($ROO) is a groundbreaking meme token built on the XRP Ledger (XRPL), designed to blend blockchain ,innovation, meme culture, and real-world impact. Our mission? To create a thriving, fun-driven crypto community while actively supporting Red Panda conservation efforts. Ripple Roo is not just a token—it’s a movement to drive financial empowerment and environmental change.

ABOUT

VISION & MISSION

Vision: To be the leading meme token on the XRP Ledger, setting the standard for fun, community engagement, and philanthropy.
Mission: To drive awareness for the XRP Ledger ecosystem while directly contributing to the preservation of Red Pandas, creating a unique, impactful crypto project.
